# Tax Document Checklist

Gather these documents before starting your tax analysis. Organize them in a single folder.

## Income Documents

- [ ] **W-2** (each employer) — Wages, withholding, FICA
  - Verify boxes 1, 2, 3-6 (SS/Medicare), 12 (retirement/health), 16-17 (state), 19 (local/CASDI)
  - For F-1/J-1: boxes 3-6 should be blank if FICA-exempt
- [ ] **1099-B** — Stock/investment sales (capital gains/losses)
  - Note: short-term vs long-term, cost basis reported vs not
- [ ] **1099-DIV** — Dividends
  - Box 1a (ordinary), 1b (qualified), 5 (Section 199A)
- [ ] **1099-INT** — Interest income
  - Box 1 (interest), 3 (US savings bonds/Treasury)
- [ ] **1042-S** — NRA income with treaty withholding
  - Income code (01=interest, 06=dividends), exemption code, tax rate, amount withheld
- [ ] **1099-MISC** — Miscellaneous income
- [ ] **1099-NEC** — Nonemployee compensation (freelance/contract)
- [ ] **1099-K** — Payment card/third-party network transactions
- [ ] **1099-R** — Retirement distributions
- [ ] **K-1** (Schedule K-1) — Partnership/S-corp/trust income

## Deduction Documents

- [ ] **1098** (Mortgage Interest Statement) — each servicer
  - Box 1 (interest paid), box 2 (outstanding principal), box 3 (origination date)
  - If loan was transferred mid-year, you'll have two 1098s — add both
- [ ] **Property tax receipts** — county tax payments
  - Include all installments paid during the calendar year
- [ ] **Vehicle registration receipt** — for VLF (Vehicle License Fee) portion
  - In CA, the VLF is the deductible portion; other fees are not
- [ ] **1098-T** (Tuition Statement) — education expenses
  - Box 1 (payments received), box 5 (scholarships)
- [ ] **1098-E** — Student loan interest
- [ ] **Charitable donation receipts** — cash and non-cash contributions
- [ ] **Closing statement** (if home purchased during year)
  - Shows property tax proration, prepaid interest, and purchase price

## Residency / Status Documents

- [ ] **Visa history** — current visa type, any status changes (dates)
- [ ] **Entry/exit records** — days present in US for last 3 years
  - Check I-94 travel history at i94.cbp.dhs.gov
- [ ] **Marriage certificate** — if married during the year (date matters)
- [ ] **Prior year tax returns** — form used (1040 or 1040-NR) and result
- [ ] **Prior year state refund amount** — taxable if you itemized last year

## Additional (If Applicable)

- [ ] **Foreign income** — wages, interest, dividends from outside the US
- [ ] **Foreign bank accounts** — for FBAR/FATCA if total exceeds $10,000
- [ ] **Health insurance** (Form 1095-A/B/C)
- [ ] **Estimated tax payments** made during the year
- [ ] **IRS notices** or correspondence
